Castalia Chronicles, Dramatis Personae

The Players

  • Aurelia Goldensong: High elf female, young adult.Cleric of Soleil, Goddess of Light and Passion. She is on her way to the main temple of Soleil in The Shining Citadel, capital of Castalia.
  • Fergus O'Toole: Human male, young adult. Bard who has been in Kyasha’s Vale for a few days, getting to know the local loggers and listening to their tales of shadows seen in the woods of late.
  • Qhask Bronzeback: Kobold ranger, young adult. Ranger. His master, the bronze dragon Caranthiax, was recently murdered by demons. He doesn't know if any other kobolds survived the assault on the dragon’s lair.
  • Vahadryl Valya Haedel: Dark elf female, young adult. Fighter, obviously not from Castalia. She doesn’t talk much about where she’s going or where she came from.

The NPCs

  • Alec Foy†: Human male, teenager, deceased. Son of William Foy, brother of Lydia Foy. Murdered by Finch in Kyasha’s Vale.
  • Arabella: Human female, adult. Priestess of Soleil in Crystalport under Cedric Daymirror.
  • Avilia Valeth: Human female, middle-aged. Owner of The Healing Hand Inn in Kyasha’s Vale. Mother of Len Valeth.
  • Beatrice: Tiefling female, young adult, deceased. Powerful priestess of Soleil. Died adventuring in the underdark in 487 A.U. Aurelia sees her in visions.
  • Bilu Kildare: Elven female, middle-aged. Master swordswoman. Teaches at the Kildare Academy in Crystalport. Valya takes lessons with her.
  • Bryn Redgate: Human male, elderly. Lord of Kyasha’s Vale, but rules in absentia from Crystalport through his reeve, Edward de Lane
  • Calvin Fentris: Human male, young adult. Younger brother of Queen Oriel Fentris. Currently hosted in Crystalport. Target of the Wood Scorpions.
  • Caranthiax†: Bronze dragon, female, ancient, deceased. Lived in the mountains northeast of Whitestone Valley. Murdered by demons. Qhask’s former master.
  • Cedric Daymirror: Human male, middle-aged. High Priest of Soleil in Crystalport. Hosting the party in the temple during their stay.
  • Darius Fentris†: Human male, elderly, deceased. Former king of Castalia. Letter found in Kyasha’s Vale was addressed to him.
  • Edward de Lane: Human male, elderly. Reeve of Kyasha’s Vale, representative of Lord Bryn Redgate. Has a nasty personality.
  • Elias Cavalier: Human male, child. Son of Maggie Cavalier. The party rescued him from shadows in the Osorin Forest.
  • Evan Woodmore: Human male, middle-aged. Captain of the Guard in Crystalport. The party asked for his aid against the hydra. Noble with no combat experience.
  • Finch Rex Hawdon†: Shapeshifter male, adult, deceased. Murdered Alec Foy in Kyasha’s Vale. Investigating the Pool of Tears. Killed by party.
  • Hamog†: Ghoul male, deceased. Encountered by the party in Vargrath Keep alongside Xatriq. Guest of Tharruk.
  • Harmony Grove: Human female, young adult. Encountered by the party in Kyasha’s Vale. Member of the Wood Scorpions. Accompanied party to Vargrath Keep.
  • Jessica: Human female, elderly. Old enough to remember some of what went on around the time the Pool of Tears went dry. Interviewed by Aurelia.
  • Kargan: Half-elven male, adult. Lieutenant under Captain Evan Woodmore. More serious and practical than the captain, and initially rude to the party.
  • Klabrog: Goliath male, adult. Leader of the town guard in Kyasha’s Vale. Gave the party a letter asking Crystalport for help with the hydra.
  • Leander: Vampire(?) male, young adult. Met by the party in Vargrath Keep after they killed Hamog and Xatriq. Orders the party to meet Tharruk.
  • Len Valeth: Human male, adult. Son of Avilia Valeth. Logger in Kyasha’s Vale. Fergus made friends with him during his stay in the village.
  • Lydia Foy: Human female, child. Daughter of William Foy, brother of Alec Foy. The party gave her water from the geode to cure her wasting disease.
  • Maggie Cavalier: Human female, middle-aged. Mother of Elias Cavalier. Lives in Crystalport. The party rescued her son from shadows in the Osorin Forest.
  • Marex: Human male, adult. Wizard working for Perrit. Member of the Wood Scorpions. Serious and intense. Cast a geas on Fergus.
  • Morgan Green: Human male, middle-aged. Sailor with ties to the Wood Scorpions. Brought the party from Crystalport to Vargrath Keep.
  • Oriel Fentris: Human female, middle-aged. Queen of Castalia, ruling from her palace in the capital. Has fired multiple ministers for mysterious reasons.
  • Perrit: Human male, middle-aged. Leader of the Wood Scorpions in Crystalport. Intense and driven. Planning to assassinate Lord Calvin Fentris with Fergus’s help.
  • Saphielle Varo: Human female, adult. Merchant and smuggler. The party met her while she was passing through Kyasha’s Vale on her way to Crystalport.
  • Toross Holaric†: Human male, deceased. A priest of Kyasha who wrote a letter to King Darius Fentris that the party discovered.
  • Trurdig: Half-orc male, adult. Saphielle Varo’s bodyguard. Met the party in Kyasha’s Vale.
  • Victoria: Human female, elderly. Met the party briefly in Greyonne. Asked the party to find her son in Silverdale. Saw an aura of death around Aurelia.
  • William Foy: Human male, middle-aged. Father of Alec and Lydia Foy. Brought his children to Kyasha’s Vale in search of a cure for his daughter.
  • Xatriq†: Skeleton female, deceased. Encountered by the party in Vargrath Keep alongside Hamog. Guest of Tharrok.
