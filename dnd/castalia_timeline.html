Timeline of Events in Castalia

652 A.U.

Tuesday November 20: Alec Foy murdered in Kyasha’s Vale. Encounter hydra near The Pool of Tears. Rex Hawdon revealed to be the killer—killed by the party while trying to escape.

Wednesday November 21: Meeting the reeve of Kyasha’s Vale, Edward de Lane. Discover secret letter that hints at caverns under the Pool of Tears. Discover an underground facility. Attacked by zombies and a gelatinous cube. Aurelia dies, but is brought back to life by the spirit of Beatrice.

Thursday November 22: Recuperating back in town. Learn about destruction of Silverdale. Jessica thinks it was divine retribution, Klabrog thinks it was necromancers in Vargrath Keep.

Friday November 23: Return to underground facility. Discover cavern with geode and hydra. Qhask finds Beatrice's amulet, hydra's scale and eggshell, but wakes hydra. Carvings on eggshell implicate followers of Neesha. Escape, get lost in mountains, make it back to Kyasha’s Vale.

Saturday November 24: Klabrog gives party letter to gain audience with Crystalport captain of the guard. Pass through Greyonne, troubling encounter with Victoria, town healer. Encounter with moorbounders. Reach Tavin, stay for the night at The Pinnacle.

Sunday November 25: Talk to tavern owner about moorbounder menace. Qhask sees creepy blindfolded woman in crowd. Travel to Crystalport. Hear about attacks on train. Speak to Cedric Daymirror, priest of Soleil, about disappearances. Speak with Evan Woodmore, captain of guard, make plans for ten days from now on Wednesday December 5. Aurelia has weird vision. Fergus secretly meets with Wood Scorpions, they want him to make distraction on Friday November 30th so they can assassinate Lord Calvin Fentris, the Queen’s younger brother.

Monday November 26: Help Maggie Cavalier find her son in the Osorin Forest. Attacked by cultist of Neesha and several shadows. Speak with Cedric about shadows. Fergus meets with Wood Scorpions again, explains insane plan.

Tuesday November 27: Harmony tells Fergus about teleportation tablet possibly in Vargrath Keep. Fergus convinces party to go under pretext of cult activity. Charter a ship to take them up the lake. Harmony accompanies. Drop anchor as close as they can get.

Wednesday November 28: Find flayed zombie with GO BACK carved into its chest. Press onwards toward Vargrath Keep. Enter third story window with grappling hook. Find letters. Fight two undead who call the lord of Vargrath Keep Tharruk. Encounter young vampire who introduces himself as Leander, Tharruk’s right-hand man.
