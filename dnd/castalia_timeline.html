Timeline of Events in Castalia

652 A.U.

Tuesday November 20: Alec Foy found murdered in Kyasha’s Vale. Party encounters hydra near Pool of Tears. Rex Hawdon, shapeshifter, revealed to be the killer.

Wednesday November 21: Meeting with Edward de Lane, Reeve of Kyasha’s Vale. Discovery of letter that leads party to secret facility under Pool of Tears. Battle with zombies and gelatinous cube. Aurelia dies and is brought back to life by Beatrice’s spirit.

Thursday November 22: Recuperate back in Kyasha’s Vale. Learn about destruction of Silverdale. Jessica blames divine retribution, Klabrog blames necromancers in Vargrath Keep.

Friday November 23: Return to the underground facility. Discover cavern with sacred geode and sleeping hydra. Qhask finds Beatrice’s amulet, hydra scale, and hydra’s eggshell, but wakes hydra in the process. Escape from the hydra, get lost in mountains, return to Kyasha’s Vale.

Saturday November 24: Klabrog gives party letter for audience with Crystalport’s Captain of the Guard. Troubling encounter with Victoria, healer of Greyonne. Encounter with moorbounders. Arrive in Tavin, rent rooms at The Pinnacle.

Sunday November 25: Talk to tavern owner about moorbounders. Qhask creeped out by blindfolded woman. Travel to Crystalport. Hear about attacks on train. Speak to Cedric Daymirror, priest of Soleil, about disappearances. Speak with Evan Woodmore, Captain of the Guard. Plan for Evan and three guardsmen to leave for Kyasha’s Vale on Wednesday December 5. Aurelia has disturbing vision. Fergus secretly meets with Wood Scorpions. Plan to distract guard on Friday November 30th so they can assassinate Lord Calvin Fentris, the Queen’s younger brother.

Monday November 26: Help find Maggie Cavalier’s son in Osorin Forest. Attacked by cultist of Neesha and several shadows. Speak with Cedric about shadows. Fergus meets with Wood Scorpions again, explains insane plan to use hydra as distraction.

Tuesday November 27: Harmony tells Fergus about a teleportation tablet possibly in Vargrath Keep. Fergus convinces party to go under pretext of cult activity. Charter a ship to take them up the lake. Harmony Grove accompanies. Drop anchor as close as they can get.

Wednesday November 28: Find flayed zombie with GO BACK carved into its chest. Press onwards toward Vargrath Keep. Enter third story window with grappling hook. Find and take stack of letters. Fight two undead who call the lord of Vargrath Keep Tharruk. Encounter Leander, vampire, Tharruk’s right-hand man.
